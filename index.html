WEAPON DETECTON AND ALERT SYSTEM USING AI

Brief...  

The Weapon Detection and Alert System leverages cutting-edge computer vision techniques to analyze live video streams or images. When a weapon—such as a firearm or knife—is detected within the camera’s field of view, the system triggers an immediate alert. These alerts can be sent to security personnel, law enforcement, or other relevant parties via notifications, emails, or alarms. By proactively identifying potential threats, this software enhances safety in public spaces, critical infrastructure, and high-risk environments. Its real-time capabilities make it a valuable tool for preventing incidents and ensuring rapid responses.
